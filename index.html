German Citizenship Test Prep

Einbürgerungstest Trainer 2026

Prepare for the German citizenship test with official questions, realistic 33-question mock tests, state-specific content, and offline study.

Einbürgerungstest Trainer helps you prepare for the German citizenship test with official question content and a focused learning experience.

Download on the App Store Google Play (Coming soon)

Features

  • Study mode with category filters
  • Includes all 310 official questions that may appear in the Einbürgerungstest, including state-specific questions
  • Official exam simulation with 33 questions per test
  • Detailed results with correct and incorrect answer review
  • Progress statistics and local test history
  • Favorites and wrong-question practice
  • Works offline for studying and test practice
  • Includes ads, with an optional one-time remove-ads purchase
  • No account or sign-up required

Language Support

For the app interface and study content:

  • German (original official question wording)
  • English
  • Turkish
  • Arabic
  • Spanish
  • Russian
  • Switch language anytime in Settings

This app is an independent learning tool and is not affiliated with any government authority.
